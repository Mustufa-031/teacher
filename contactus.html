PHASE 01 // ORBITAL ALIGNMENT

The Cosmic Dance

A solar eclipse occurs when the Moon passes between the Sun and Earth, casting a shadow on Earth. This can only happen during a New Moon, when the Sun and Moon are conjunct in the sky.

PHASE 02 // UMBRA & PENUMBRA

The Shadow Cone

The Moon's shadow has two parts: the dark central umbra, which causes total eclipses, and the lighter outer penumbra, which causes partial eclipses. We are tracking the umbra.

PHASE 03 // SECOND CONTACT

The Diamond Ring

Just before totality, the last rays of sunlight stream through the lunar valleys, creating a brilliant burst of light. This is the Diamond Ring effect, signaling that totality is imminent.

PHASE 04 // TOTALITY

The Corona Revealed

The Sun is entirely obscured. The solar corona—normally invisible—blazes into view. The sky darkens to twilight, and the temperature drops. This phase lasts mere minutes.
